About Bill Griffin Motors

Here at Bill Griffin Motors we pride ourselves on being family run for over thirty years and being one of Ireland's largest used car dealers with over 20,000 cars sold in that time, all from our three floor Naas road showroom (you will get directions to our garage here). Each of our 300+ cars are in stock today and come fully instected, history checked and warrantied.

With over 40,000 Happy Customers over the past 30 years you can be assured of a unrivalled quality purchase.

As the most innovative car dealership in Ireland we are proud to now offer used car PCP rates. We pride ourselves on hassle free, professional selling and on undercutting the market which we can do due to our huge buying power. We are members of the SIMI and offer excellent trade-in deals. We also work with all major banks and are an AIB and Bank of Ireland approved dealer. New stock arrives daily so please check back regularly. Thank you for visiting our site.
